About Kratex

Kratex is a Mumbai born DJ and producer shaping a new wave of electronic music by fusing Indian cultural identity with cutting edge house and techno. As the pioneer of "M House" a genre that blends Marathi elements with global club sounds he is redefining how regional music can exist on the international stage.

His breakout track "Taambdi Chaamdi" released on Spinnin' Records has crossed 100 million streams marking a milestone for Indian language electronic music globally. The record's success positioned Kratex as one of the first artists to take Marathi rooted sounds into the mainstream global dance ecosystem.

A proven live force Kratex has consistently delivered large scale headline performances. His MVP Music Festival drew a crowd of 6000 plus attendees establishing him as one of the few independent electronic artists in India capable of building and commanding festival scale audiences. He has also pulled 2500 plus attendees at Club Ballr Pune setting a new benchmark for club shows in the country. His performances combine high impact drops with a strong visual identity anchored by his signature triangle symbol.

With millions plus streams across platforms Kratex continues to build a sound that travels beyond borders while staying rooted in culture. His growing presence across India Southeast Asia and key global dance markets reflects a clear mission not just to participate in the global scene but to export a new cultural sound from India to the world.

Kratex has gained good support and appreciation on his productions from Afrojack, Chris Lake, Claude Von Stroke, Carnage, Fedde le Grand, Timmy Trumpet, Blasterjaxx, Club Banditz, etc
